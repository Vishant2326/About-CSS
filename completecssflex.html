CSS Flexbox Cheat Sheet

Flex Container Properties

Property Description Values
display Sets the display type of the container flex | inline-flex
flex-direction Sets the direction of the main axis row | row-reverse | column | column-reverse
flex-wrap Sets whether items should wrap or not nowrap | wrap | wrap-reverse
justify-content Sets the alignment of items along the main axis flex-start | flex-end | center | space-between | space-around | space-evenly
align-items Sets the alignment of items along the cross axis flex-start | flex-end | center | baseline | stretch
align-content Sets the alignment of lines along the cross axis flex-start | flex-end | center | space-between | space-around | stretch
